Every day we are faced with the stark reality that we, as a people, are failing our planet and sabotaging our future. As global temperatures rise, human suffering increases, landfills overflow, coral reefs are dying, infant mortality rates are skyrocketing, and our natural resources are depleting, the human race is being called upon to make serious systemic, societal, and behavioral changes that could well determine our ability to survive as a species.

At Nemesis Vs (Perennial Enterprise's Inc), we believe each of us holds a direct moral obligation to face down injustice, reform policy, support just causes, and demand change – all in the name of our unified global survival.

About Our Founder

Growing up in Compton, California, Reginald Morgan has long been worried about the state of our country, our humanity, and our world. With a passion for fighting injustice and defending our planet, Reginald established Nemesis Vs (Perennial Enterprise's Inc) to do his part to cultivate lasting change and lead a movement for other eco-minded, social justice driven, and equality focused individuals to unite and protect our dying planet and it’s inhabitants. For over four years, Reginald has worked two jobs for sixteen hours a day in order to facilitate the lasting change he knows is necessary for our survival, specifically focusing his energy in his passion eradicating homelessness, healing the coral reefs, and fighting injustice in all forms.
